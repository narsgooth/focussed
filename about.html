Anna Oop

 Bio
anna oop (born: May 12, 2019 [age 22]) is an American YouTuber and social media personality who rose to fame by publishing influencer and celebrity gossip videos on her anna oop channel, with some celebrities being talked about including Eugenia Cooney and Charli D'Amelio. As of September 2021, she has almost two million subscribers.

Alternate channels
anna solves is Anna's mystery channel. It was created back on Oct. 5, 2018, but its first video was uploaded a full 3 years later. It covers true crime stories, however only the ones left unsolved. anna horror stories are Anna's true crime channel. In this channel, she talks about urban legends, true crime stories, and creepy stories that may or may not be true. anna plays is Anna's gaming channel. She created the channel on October7, 2021, and games on this channel, usually she plays the game ROBLOX. As of right now, the channel uploads once every few days.

Conspiracy Theories
Some people have claimed that Anna isn't a real person and that she uses a voice changer or AI. Some also think that she is backed by a social media company targeted at Gen Z.

Anna oop made a video on That Vegan Teacher at some point, and TVT's response was calling Anna an 'animal abuser' and since then, the two have had beef with one another. TVT also frequently targets Anna for Anna's videos on V-gan Booty, and even made a song calling her a 'Veganphobic poop'. Anna has stayed calm throughout the feud, but she did release a song called "FACE REVEAL" that features lyrics that diss TVT.
